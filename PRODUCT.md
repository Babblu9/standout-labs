# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Founders, marketing leads, and business owners — many searching locally ("digital marketing in Hyderabad") but also national/global. They arrive wanting growth: a brand that stands out, a site that converts, marketing that works, and AI/automation that gives them leverage. Context: evaluating agencies, skeptical of hype, short on time. They need to feel this studio is both creative and technically serious in the first scroll.

## Product Purpose

StandoutLabs is a full-service creative + growth studio: digital marketing, AI branding, web, content, growth, and automation. The site exists to (1) rank for high-intent local searches like "digital marketing in Hyderabad" and (2) convert that traffic by proving the studio can make a brand stand out. Success = qualified inbound project inquiries. The site itself is the primary proof of craft — it must practice what it preaches.

## Brand Personality

Bold, confident, premium. Voice is direct and self-assured, never salesy or hype-y. Shows capability through the craft of the page itself rather than claiming it. Emotional goal: the visitor feels they've found operators who are a level above the local field.

## Anti-references

- **Generic AI/SaaS templates** — no hero-metric blocks, no identical icon-card grids, no gradient text, no tiny uppercase tracked eyebrows above every section.
- **Cheap local-agency sites** — no stock photos, no keyword-stuffed footers, no spammy "#1 in Hyderabad" badges, no cluttered service lists.
- **Corporate/boring** — not stiff navy-and-gray consultancy. Must feel bold and premium, with intentional motion.

## Design Principles

1. **Practice what you preach** — the page is the portfolio. Every detail is proof the studio can make a brand stand out.
2. **Show, don't tell** — demonstrate capability through craft and interaction, not adjectives.
3. **Local intent, global craft** — win the Hyderabad search without looking like a local-agency site.
4. **Motion earns its place** — every animation serves comprehension or delight; nothing moves for its own sake, and nothing is slow.
5. **Structured, not sprawling** — clear hierarchy and rhythm; a visitor always knows where they are and what to do next.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA. Body text ≥4.5:1 contrast. Full `prefers-reduced-motion` alternatives for every scroll/entrance animation. Keyboard-navigable nav and interactive demos. Custom cursor is a desktop-only enhancement layered over native pointer behavior, never a replacement.
