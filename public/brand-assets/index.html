Notes

SVGs are the master files — all PNG/WebP are rendered from them with transparent backgrounds at 512 / 1024 / 2048 / 4096 px. Text-bearing SVGs (wordmark & lockups) embed a Space Grotesk web-font import so they render standalone in browsers, Framer, and Rive. For After Effects / Lottie or any offline pipeline, outline the type on import (or use the mark-only files, which are pure vector shapes and need no font). The blue-square.svg is isolated as your reusable hero element for cursors, transitions, loaders, and compositing.
